Thousands of people trust STEMist. Here's Why.

STEMist is a 501 (C) (3) certified nonprofit organization run by qualified high school students, who have obtained medals in several academic competitions. Our top priority is student satisfaction, and we are dedicated to ensuring that all participants enjoy the events.

Sponsors

Interview Cake

STEMist Mathathon Sponsor

Interview Cake is an interactive online platform designed to help software engineers prepare for technical interviews. They offer a structured approach to learning key data structures and algorithms, along with practice questions and detailed explanations. We are incredibly grateful for their generous contribution of over $2500 in prizes for the STEMist Mathathon, which will greatly benefit our participants!

Art of Problem Solving (AoPS)

STEMist Mathathon Sponsor

Art of Problem Solving (AoPS) is a renowned leader in mathematics education, offering a comprehensive curriculum, engaging online courses, and a vibrant community for students passionate about mathematics. Their resources are highly respected and have helped countless students excel in math competitions and develop a deep understanding of the subject.

We are immensely thankful for their generous monetary donation to the STEMist Mathathon, which will significantly enhance the event and support our participants' learning journey.

Wolfram

STEMist Mathathon Sponsor

Wolfram is a world-renowned leader in computation and knowledge, widely recognized for Mathematica, Wolfram|Alpha, and the Wolfram Language. Their tools and technologies are pivotal in advancing research, education, and innovation across numerous scientific and technical fields. We are extremely grateful for Wolfram's valuable contribution to the STEMist Mathathon, which will provide significant resources and opportunities for our participants.

Jane Street

STEMist Mathathon Sponsor

Jane Street is a quantitative trading firm known for its deep engagement with the mathematical and computer science communities. They are recognized for their support of prestigious mathematics competitions such as the Stanford Math Tournament, Caltech Math Meet, and Berkeley Math Tournament, fostering talent and enthusiasm for quantitative problem-solving.

We are incredibly appreciative of Jane Street's generous sponsorship of the STEMist Mathathon, which will greatly enhance the experience for all our participants and help us promote a passion for mathematics.
