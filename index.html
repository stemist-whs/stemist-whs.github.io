Thousands of people trust STEMist. Here's Why.

STEMist is a 501 (C) (3) certified nonprofit organization run by qualified high school students, who
have obtained medals in several academic competitions. Our top priority is student satisfaction, and
we are dedicated to ensuring that all participants enjoy the events.

Welcome to the STEMist Mathathon!

The STEMist Mathathon is a prestigious team-based and engaging math competition in which elementary and middle-school students have the opportunity to solve complex problems that require critical thinking far beyond the average school curriculum. Participants compete with other talented peers in rounds that challenge their mathematical, collaborative, and logical thinking abilities. In addition to fostering academic growth, the STEMist Mathathon emphasizes the importance of teamwork and creative problem-solving. By participating, students gain valuable experience that will benefit them in future academic endeavors and beyond.
